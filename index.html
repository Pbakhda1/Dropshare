Safety & rules

Keep sharing helpful and safe content.

Allowed

  • Files you have rights to share
  • Free/open-source apps and resources
  • Local community docs and learning materials

Not allowed

  • Pirated/copyrighted downloads without permission
  • Malware, phishing links, suspicious installers
  • Passwords/IDs/private data dumps

Production upgrades

  • Accounts + secure storage + antivirus scanning
  • Moderation/reporting + reputation
  • Privacy-first location controls
Note: This demo is local-only. Real sharing requires backend, storage, scanning, and moderation.

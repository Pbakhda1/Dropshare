Rules

Keep DropShare safe, respectful, and legal.

Allowed

  • Files you own or have permission to share
  • Work documents/resources meant for your team
  • Free/open-source apps and trusted links

Not allowed

  • Pirated copyrighted content
  • Malware, phishing, suspicious installers
  • Sharing private data without consent

Safety

  • Only download from trusted senders
  • Prefer QR over sound in noisy places
  • Use moderation for public communities
